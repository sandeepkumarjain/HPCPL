About Us

Integrated Capability. Disciplined Execution.

Hannath Group has grown from a focused trading operation into a fully integrated enterprise spanning sourcing, imports, logistics, decanting, nationwide distribution, and digital customer support.

Established in 1997, the group has built its reputation through dependable supply, structured commercial conduct, and an operating culture shaped by responsiveness and trust. Today, HANNANTH PETRO CHEM PRIVATE LIMITED serves contractors, corporates, infrastructure developers, and project-led buyers who value continuity and execution.
