About Hannath Group

Dependable Growth, Built Through Execution

Hannath Group is one of India's dependable and execution-focused organizations in the bitumen ecosystem, serving infrastructure buyers with sourcing strength, logistics discipline, and nationwide supply support.

Who We Are

Integrated across sourcing, imports, logistics, and supply movement.

Established in 1997, the group has evolved from a focused trading operation into a fully integrated enterprise delivering sourcing, imports, logistics, decanting, nationwide distribution, and digital support across India.

Driven by discipline, market understanding, and an uncompromising commitment to delivery, Hannath Group has positioned itself as a trusted partner for contractors, infrastructure developers, corporates, and government projects.
